Unlocking the Path to Quranic Reading: The Noorani Qaida Course

Noorani Qaida is a foundational course that serves as a stepping stone for anyone eager to read the Quran correctly. It is a timeless resource that has been used for generations to teach Arabic script and Tajweed principles. The word "Noorani" itself means "bright" or "enlightening," which aptly describes the illumination of knowledge that this course imparts.

Noorani Qaida begins with the basics, teaching you to recognize and write the Arabic alphabet. This foundational knowledge is essential for Quranic reading and comprehension. Tajweed is the correct pronunciation of Quranic verses. Noorani Qaida delves into Tajweed rules, ensuring that you can read the Quran with proper articulation, rhythm, and melody.

The course progresses from basic alphabets to words and sentences, gradually building your reading skills. This gradual approach ensures comprehension and fluency. As you master each lesson, your confidence in reading the Quran grows. This newfound confidence deepens your connection with the sacred text.

We start with the fundamentals by teaching you how to recognize and write each letter of the Arabic alphabet. You'll learn how vowel marks, or harakat, affect the pronunciation of words and how to apply them correctly. Understanding the concept of sukun (non-vowelization) and its significance in Quranic reading.

 

Delving into the rules of Tajweed, such as the characteristics of letters, proper pronunciation, and pauses in recitation. Regular practice sessions and exercises to reinforce your learning and enhance your reading skills.

We employ modern teaching techniques and interactive tools to make learning engaging and enjoyable. Our online lectures are delivered through WhatsApp,Skype, and Zoom.
